Whale Behavior Analysis in Cryptocurrency Markets

Unveiling Whale Behavior by Clustering, Anomaly Detection, and Price Prediction in Crypto Markets

üìå Project Overview

The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile, influenced by whales (large-scale holders) who execute high-value transactions. Their movements can significantly impact prices, causing rapid fluctuations.
This project examined whale behavior, detected anomalous transactions, and builded predictive models to assess their effect on Ethereum (ETH) prices.

üéØ Objectives

  • Behavioral Analysis: Classify whale behavior patterns using clustering algorithms and network graph analysis.
  • Anomaly Detection: Detect anomalies and identify suspicious transactions using outlier detection techniques.
  • Impact Assessment: Analyze the impact of whale trades on Ethereum prices using statistical modeling and predictive analytics.
